### 发布日志（中文）

# Obsidian ai侧边栏

在 Obsidian 侧边栏中聚合多个在线 AI 平台和搜索引擎。

<img width="750" height="652" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/04ade162-fd8f-46af-b712-52f98a9bbeab" />


## ✨ 功能

- **12 个内置 AI**：DeepSeek、Kimi、豆包、千问、元宝、文心一言、Copilot、Z.ai、Gemini、ChatGPT、Claude、Grok
- **搜索与直达**：Google / Bing 搜索，或输入任意网址直接访问
- **拖拽排序**：首页 AI 卡片和标签栏均支持拖拽调整顺序
- **主题适配**：自动跟随 Obsidian 浅色 / 深色主题
- **多语言**：中文 / English

## 📦 安装

### 简便安装

1. 在release页面下载ai-sidebar.zip
2. 解压后将ai-sidebar文件夹放入 .obsidian/plugins/ 文件夹
3. 重启 Obsidian 后启用

### 手动安装

1. 下载 `main.js`、`manifest.json`、`styles.css`
2. 放入 Vault 目录：`.obsidian/plugins/ai-sidebar/`
3. 在 Obsidian 设置中启用插件

## 🚀 使用

1. 点击左侧工具栏 ✈️ 图标打开 AI Sidebar
2. 在首页点击 AI 卡片打开对应平台
3. 顶部搜索栏可搜索或输入网址
4. 长按拖拽 AI 卡片调整顺序
5. 点击标签 × 关闭，未关闭的标签下次自动恢复

## ⚙️ 设置

- **主题模式**：Light / Dark / System
- **界面语言**：中文 / English
- **搜索引擎**：Google / Bing
- **恢复默认排序**：重置 AI 卡片顺序

### Release Notes (English)

# Obsidian AI Sidebar

Aggregate multiple online AI platforms and search engines in Obsidian's sidebar.

<img width="750" height="652" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/04ade162-fd8f-46af-b712-52f98a9bbeab" />

## Features

- **12 Built-in AI Platforms**: DeepSeek, Kimi, Doubao, Qwen, Yuanbao, Wenxin, Copilot, Z.ai, Gemini, ChatGPT, Claude, Grok
- **Search & Direct URL**: Google / Bing search, or enter any URL directly
- **Drag to Reorder**: Reorder AI cards on the home page and tabs in the tab bar
- **Theme Adaptation**: Follows Obsidian light / dark theme
- **Bilingual**: English / 中文

## Installation

###Simple Installation
1. Download `ai-sidebar.zip` from the release page

2. After extracting, place the `ai-sidebar` folder into the `.obsidian/plugins/` folder

3. Restart Obsidian and enable the plugin

### Manual Install

1. Download `main.js`, `manifest.json`, `styles.css`
2. Place in your vault: `.obsidian/plugins/ai-sidebar/`
3. Enable the plugin in Obsidian settings

## Usage

1. Click the ✈️ ribbon icon to open AI Sidebar
2. Click any AI card on the home page to open it
3. Use the search bar to search or enter a URL
4. Long-press and drag AI cards to reorder
5. Click × on a tab to close; unclosed tabs auto-restore on next launch

## Settings

- **Theme mode**: Light / Dark / System
- **Language**: English / 中文
- **Search engine**: Google / Bing
- **Restore default order**: Reset AI card order
